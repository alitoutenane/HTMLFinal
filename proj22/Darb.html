Students face many challenges when choosing their academic major

Example: A student who is creatively gifted in art but chooses engineering under the pressure of the belief that scientific fields are more successful.

We provide you with an overview of different majors to help you make an informed decision,
but remember: the final choice is yours.
Choose what aligns with your passion and capabilities, and know that success begins when you trust your abilities!

1. College of Business Administration :

Major in Supply Chain Management

About the Major :
Focuses on planning and organizing the flow of goods from producer to consumer while optimizing costs and time.Highly sought-after in manufacturing and e-commerce companies
(for example : Amazon, Alibaba).

2.College of Arts :

Major in Digital Arts & Multimedia

About the Major :
Combines artistic creativity with modern technology to produce visual and interactive content
(for example : animations, video games, short films).
One of the fastest-growing fields due to the digital revolution and rising demand for engaging content in advertising, cinema, and social platforms.

3.College of Engineering :

Major in Biomedical Engineering :

About the Major :
Combines engineering and medical sciences to design medical devices
(for example : prosthetics, medical imaging equipment).
Has a promising future due to technological advancements in the healthcare sector.

4. College of Computer Science :

Major in Cybersecurity :

About the Major :
Focuses on protecting systems and networks from breaches and cyberattacks.
One of the most globally in-demand fields due to the rise in cybercrime.

5. College of Business Administration :

Major in Digital Marketing :

About the Major :
Focuses on online marketing strategies (for example : social media, data analytics, paid advertising).
In demand across all industries due to the global digital transformation.
